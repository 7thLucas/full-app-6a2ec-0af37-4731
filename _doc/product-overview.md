# Trading Academy — Product Overview

## Product
**Name:** Trading Academy  
**Tagline:** Learn Trading Safely  
**Platform:** Flutter (mobile — iOS & Android)

## Mission
Empower everyday people to understand and practice trading through structured, risk-free education — before they ever commit real capital.

## Audience

### Primary: Beginner Traders
People curious about financial markets who have little to no prior trading experience. They want to learn but fear losing real money. They need guidance, safety rails, and a clear path from zero to competent.

### Secondary: Self-Directed Learners
Individuals already investing who want to sharpen strategy, understand markets more deeply, and practice new approaches without putting their existing portfolio at risk.

## Positioning
A mobile-first trading education app — not a brokerage, not a signal service. Purely educational. Trading Academy's promise is that users learn the right way: structured, safe, and at their own pace.

## Brand & Tone
- **Trustworthy & calm:** Finance is high-stakes; the app feels reassuring, not anxiety-inducing
- **Encouraging:** Every learner starts somewhere; the tone lifts rather than intimidates
- **Professional but approachable:** Credible enough for serious learners, clear enough for complete beginners

## Initial Scope (MVP)
- Structured lesson modules covering market basics, chart reading, and trading strategy
- Risk-free practice environment — no real money involved at any point
- Progressive learning paths from beginner to confident trader
- Guided exercises and scenario-based challenges
- Learner progress tracking with milestones
- Mobile-first experience built with Flutter

## Strategic Principles
1. **Safety first** — zero real financial risk at every interaction
2. **Progressive learning** — structured pathways that build confidence step by step
3. **Practice over theory** — guided exercises and simulations reinforce every concept
4. **Accessible to all** — no prior knowledge required to start

## Out of Scope (Initial)
- Live trading or brokerage integration
- Real-money transactions of any kind
- Signal or tip services
